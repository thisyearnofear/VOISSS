## How to Collect Customer Evidence

1. **Email request** — Send a short email to customers asking for a 1-2 sentence testimonial
2. **In-app prompt** — After a successful voice generation, show a "How was your experience?" dialog
3. **Telegram/Discord** — Ask in your community channels
4. **Twitter/X** — Monitor mentions and reach out to positive commenters

**Template email:**
```
Subject: Quick question about VOISSS

Hi [Name],

Thanks for using VOISSS! We're participating in a hackathon that requires
customer feedback. Would you be open to sharing a quick 1-2 sentence
testimonial about your experience?

Even just "It worked and was easy to use" helps us a lot.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```
